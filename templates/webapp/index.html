Ask Us Anything

Duostore is a platform that connects local vendors with nearby customers through online and offline solutions, making it easy to find and purchase products locally.

Simply sign up on our website, complete the vendor profile, and submit for verification. Once approved, you can start listing products and reach local customers.

We support a wide range of categories, from groceries and electronics to fashion and home essentials. Check our guidelines for any specific category restrictions.

Customers can search by product or category, and Duostore uses location-based features to show your products to customers nearby, helping you connect with your community.

Yes, we charge a small listing fee and a transaction fee per sale to cover platform maintenance and marketing efforts. Specific fee details can be found in our Vendor Terms.
